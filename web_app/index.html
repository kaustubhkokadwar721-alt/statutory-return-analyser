    Private by design

    Your documents stay on this device.

    The analyser is downloaded to your browser. When you add a PDF, it moves only into the browser's working memory. There is no document upload and no server-side processing.

    1. Add PDFs. They remain in this browser tab.
    2. Run the analyser. WebAssembly and local OCR use your computer's processor.
    3. Download Excel. The workbook is created locally for you.
    4. Close the tab. The working document data is discarded.
    The analyser keeps working after the first load even when the internet is disconnected.
